## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1.  Navigate to the [registration page](/authentication/register).
2.  Fill in the required details, including your name, email address, and password.
3.  Choose your account type: Customer, Rider, or Merchant.
4.  Click "Register" to create your account.

### Logging In

1.  Navigate to the [login page](/authentication/login).
2.  Enter your registered email and password.
3.  Click "Login" to access your dashboard.

## For Customers

As a customer, you can easily place orders and track them in real-time.

### Placing an Order

1.  From your dashboard, navigate to the "Explore" or "Products" page.
2.  Browse through the available products and add items to your cart.
3.  Proceed to checkout.
4.  Enter the delivery address and any specific instructions.
5.  Confirm your order and proceed to payment.

### Tracking Your Delivery

1.  Once your order is placed, you can track its status in the "Orders" section of your dashboard.
2.  You will see real-time updates as the rider picks up and delivers your package.
3.  You will receive notifications at every stage of the delivery process.

### Managing Your Profile

-   You can update your personal information and view your order history in the "Profile" section.

## For Riders

As a rider, you are the backbone of our delivery service.

### Becoming a Rider

1.  Register as a "Rider" on the registration page.
2.  Complete your profile by providing the necessary verification documents.
3.  Once your application is reviewed and approved by an administrator, you can start accepting deliveries.

### Accepting and Managing Deliveries

1.  From your rider dashboard, you can view available delivery requests.
2.  Accept a delivery that suits you.
3.  Follow the pickup and drop-off instructions.
4.  Update the delivery status in the app as you complete each stage.

## For Merchants

As a merchant, you can sell your products on our platform.

### Setting Up Your Store

1.  Register as a "Merchant" and complete the verification process.
2.  Once approved, you can set up your company profile, including your store name, logo, and pickup locations.

### Managing Products

-   In your merchant dashboard, you can add, edit, and remove products.
-   Set your own prices and manage your inventory.

### Viewing Orders

-   You will be notified of new orders for your products.
-   Track the status of orders and manage your sales from the "Orders" section.

## For Administrators

As an administrator, you have oversight over the entire platform.

### Dashboard Overview

-   The admin dashboard provides a comprehensive view of all users, orders, and platform activity.

### Managing Users

-   You can view, edit, and manage all user accounts (Customers, Riders, and Merchants).

### Approving Riders and Merchants

-   Review and approve or decline applications from new riders and merchants.
-   Ensure all necessary verification documents are in order.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How do I reset my password?**

A: On the login page, click the "Forgot Password" link and follow the instructions to reset your password.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**

A: We accept a variety of payment methods, including M-Pesa and major credit/debit cards.

**Q: How is my data protected?**

A: We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All payments are processed through a secure gateway.
